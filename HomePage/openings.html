Open Positions

I am looking for motivated students interested in machine learning and optimization, especially reliable artificial intelligence. I am currently interested in the following topics:
  • Robustness against adaptive and general adversarial perturbations

  • Differentiable privacy and its relationship with robustness and generalization.

  • Optimization and generalization properties of deep learning.
I have several openings as follows. We offer competitive salaries.

Ph.D. Students

If you are interested in doing a Ph.D. at the City University of Hong Kong and working with me, you can apply in one of the following ways:
  • Submit an application. If you are interested in working with me, mention me in your research statement.

  • Apply directly by sending me an email with your CV and your undergraduate transcript. Please tell me your past research project and what you expect about doing a Ph.D.
Successful applicants are eligible to apply for Hong Kong Ph.D Fellowship Scheme. Ph.D. students can be enrolled in January, June and September each year.

Research Assistant

If you are a Bachelor or Master student interested in doing an internship as research assistant, please send me an email with your CV.
