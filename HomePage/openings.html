Open Positions

I am looking for motivated Ph.D. students in the year of 2024 interested in machine learning and optimization, especially in the field of reliable machine learning. I am currently interested in the following topics:
  • Robustness against adaptive and general adversarial perturbation with applications in auto-driving.

  • Effective and efficient algorithms to train deep neural networks with guarantees in differentiable privacy.

  • Optimization and generalization properties of deep learning.

Applicants should have a bachelor degree in computer science, mathematics or related majors, with a GPA higher than 85 / 100 or 3.3 / 4.0 (80 / 100, 3.0 / 4.0 for a few top universities).

If you are interested in working with me, apply directly by sending me an email with your CV and your undergraduate transcript. Outstanding candidates are eligible to apply for Hong Kong Ph.D Fellowship Scheme, in which a higher rate of scholarship (~27.6k HKD per month) will be provided.

Ph.D. students can be enrolled in January, June and September each year.
