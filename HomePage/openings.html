Open Positions

I am looking for motivated research students in the year of 2024 interested in machine learning and optimization, especially in the field of reliable machine learning. I am currently interested in the following topics:
  • Robustness against adaptive and general adversarial perturbation with applications in auto-driving.

  • Effective and efficient algorithms to train deep neural networks with guarantees in differentiable privacy.

  • Optimization and generalization properties of deep learning.

Applicants should have a bachelor degree in computer science, mathematics or related majors, with a GPA higher than 85 / 100 or 3.3 / 4.0 (80 / 100, 3.0 / 4.0 for a few top universities). Other requirements upon application are listed on the university's website.

If you are interested in working with me, apply directly by sending me an email with your CV and your undergraduate transcript.

For successful Ph.D. applicants, the studentship rate is arond 18k HKD per month. You can check this webpage for more information about the financial aid provided by the university. Outstanding candidates are eligible to apply for Hong Kong Ph.D Fellowship Scheme, in which a higher rate of scholarship (~27.6k HKD per month) will be provided.

City University of Hong Kong also have joint Ph.D. programmes with other universities. The entrance requirements are generally the same as regular Ph.D. students. More information is available on this website.

Research students can be enrolled in January, June and September each year.
