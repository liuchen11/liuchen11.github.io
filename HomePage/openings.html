Open Positions

I am looking for motivated students and postdocs interested in machine learning and optimization, especially reliable artificial intelligence. I am currently interested in the following topics:
  • Efficient adversarial training to improve adversarial robustness.

  • Adaptive robustness against different categories of attacks.

  • Optimization and generalization properties of existing robust learning methods.
I have several openings as follows. We offer competitive salaries.

Ph.D Students

If you are interested in doing a Ph.D at the City University of Hong Kong, you can:
  1. Submit an application. If you are interested in working with me, mention me in your research statement.

  2. Apply directly by sending me an email with you CV and research statement. Please tell me your past research project and what you expect about doing a Ph.D.
Successful applicants are eligible to apply for Hong Kong Ph.D Fellowship Scheme. Ph.D students can be enrolled in the beginning of each semester. There are two normal semesters each year, starting at early Januaray and late August, respectively.
